Tuna Corn Cheese Buldak Noodles

Home

This is another quick, easy, affordable meal that will take you only minutes to make. There are many popular Buldak (Korean spicy chicken-flavored noodles) recipes and combinations out there, but this one with tuna is delicious and adds protein to these instant noodles. Add corn and cheese, and you have an incredibly filling meal.


Makes 1 Serving

Ingredients

    1 package buldak noodles of your choice
    1 can of tuna
    ½ can of corn
    ¼ cup of mozzarella cheese
  1. Prepare buldak noodles according to instructions. Set aside toppings packet.
  2. Add tuna and corn. Mix tuna and corn into the buldak noodles after it is completed.
  3. Top buldak with cheese and toppings packet.
  4. Enjoy!
